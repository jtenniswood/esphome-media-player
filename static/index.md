A touchscreen media controller for Home Assistant, displays album art, track info, and provides touch controls for any media player in your smart home.

Connect the Guition ESP32-S3-4848S040 to your computer via USB-C, then click the button below to install.

<esp-web-install-button manifest="./firmware/media-player.manifest.json"></esp-web-install-button>

**Requires Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge on desktop.**

<script type="module" src="https://unpkg.com/esp-web-tools@10/dist/web/install-button.js?module"></script>

Once installed, follow the [Install Guide](https://github.com/jtenniswood/esphome-media-player?tab=readme-ov-file#install-guide) to connect to WiFi, adopt the device in Home Assistant, select your media player.
