# Install

*A touchscreen media controller for Home Assistant, displays album art, track info, and provides touch controls for any media player in your smart home.*

**Supported devices:** [Guition ESP32-S3 4848S040 (4")](/devices/esp32-s3-4848s040) · [Guition ESP32-P4 JC8012P4A1 (8")](/devices/esp32-p4-jc8012p4a1)

Connect your panel to your computer via USB-C, then click **Install** below. The installer will offer the correct firmware for your device.

<ClientOnly>
  <InstallButton />
  <template #fallback>
    <p><em>Loading installer…</em></p>
  </template>
</ClientOnly>

Once installed, follow the [Install Guide](https://github.com/jtenniswood/esphome-media-player?tab=readme-ov-file#install-guide) to connect to WiFi, adopt the device in Home Assistant, and select your media player.
